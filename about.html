Background

I'm a Business Operations & Revenue Coordinator at ProWeb Limited, where I lead group-level operations across multiple business units. My work sits at the intersection of process design, CRM optimisation, and cross-functional project delivery — building systems that make complex organisations run cleanly.

Over 4+ years I've moved from operations associate to coordinator, taking on progressively wider scope across revenue operations, vendor management, QA, and executive reporting. I've had the privilege of working on digital platforms for some of Zambia's most prominent institutions — the central bank, capital markets regulators, and investment holding companies.

I hold a BBA (Cum Laude, 3.54 GPA, Dean's List every semester) from the University of the People — a WSCUC-accredited institution — and carry dual HubSpot certifications in Revenue Operations and Sales Hub Software.
